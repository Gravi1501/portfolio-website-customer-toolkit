Executive Overview

Welcome to my IT Supervisor Readiness Web Portfolio. This site was developed as part of my academic capstone for the Information Technology Field Experience course at Cumberland University. It showcases my readiness to lead, mentor, and manage within technical teams by integrating both human-centered leadership practices and structured IT operations. The tools and frameworks included here were designed to reflect real-world practices in people management, strategic communication, and technology service delivery.

Leadership Vision

As an aspiring IT Supervisor, I am committed to leading with a strategic mindset, operational clarity, and unwavering ethics. My leadership approach focuses on building resilient teams that can respond effectively to change while promoting innovation, integrity, and collaboration. I believe in leading by example—driving accountability, supporting growth, and aligning every decision with long-term organizational success. This vision is reflected across the leadership and HR tools presented in this portfolio.

Key Portfolio Tools

This portfolio contains four foundational tools aligned with industry standards from SHRM, PMI, and Harkins’ “Protect to Enable” strategy:

  • 3-Day Onboarding Plan: A structured integration plan for new IT hires that covers orientation, access setup, role immersion, and KPI introduction (SHRM, 2024).
  • RACI Chart: A responsibility matrix for common IT workflows, including system patching, onboarding, and incident escalation (PMI, 2023).
  • PTO Tracker: A resource allocation tracker that helps ensure service continuity while honoring staff leave and availability (Bersin by Deloitte, 2022).
  • HR Memo Template: A structured and compliant tool to document and resolve behavioral or policy violations (Harvard Business Review, 2022).

Website Architecture

This portfolio is organized with ease of use and professionalism in mind. The navigation bar provides direct access to each major leadership resource. Each file has been published in an accessible format (PDF, DOCX, XLSX) and can be downloaded or previewed for review. Sections include:

  • Homepage: Executive summary, leadership philosophy, and professional branding.
  • Team Leadership Toolkit: Tools for onboarding, task accountability, and HR documentation.
  • Resume & Contact: Downloadable résumé and contact form for professional networking.
