3. Sample Helpdesk Ticket Response

Subject: Re: Email Not Functioning – Urgent Ticket

Dear [User Name],

Thank you for reporting the issue. I understand how essential email functionality is to your daily work and apologize for the inconvenience.

It appears the problem is linked to mailbox synchronization or server timeout. I have escalated this to Tier 2 Support for further diagnostics. Meanwhile, please use Outlook Web Access [insert link] as a temporary solution.

You’ll receive an update within the next hour.

Best regards,
Sai Ravi Teja Garikipati
IT Support Supervisor
