This professional toolkit showcases my strategy for handling IT service management, complaint resolution, and helpdesk escalations as part of my ISR Web Portfolio project. The following components represent industry-standard practices guided by frameworks like ITIL and HDI.

1. Customer Service Standards Statement

A one-page document describing my personal customer service philosophy, including how IT teams should treat end users, define great service, and handle complaints with professionalism.
